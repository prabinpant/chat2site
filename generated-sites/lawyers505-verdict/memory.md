# Lawyers 505 Memory

## INTERNAL STRATEGY

### Project Status
Fresh project. No new site has been built yet.

### The Vision
Create a refactored website for Lawyers 505 that feels more credible, current, and decisive than the existing site while preserving the firm's strongest real-world strengths from the source material: courtroom experience, aggressive advocacy, broad New Mexico reach, direct consultation access, and clear coverage of divorce, criminal defense, personal injury, and family law.

The finished site should feel like a serious Albuquerque trial firm: confident, high-trust, plainspoken, and action-oriented. It should not feel corporate-polished to the point of losing grit, and it should not feel dated or cluttered like a legacy brochure site.

### Brand Identity
Lawyers 505 is a New Mexico law firm centered in Albuquerque. The existing site repeatedly leans on these messages:

- Experience | Commitment | Results
- We Know What it Takes to Win
- Smart, aggressive, effective representation
- Over 25 years of courtroom trial experience
- Free consultations, same-day appointments, payment plans
- Serving Albuquerque and clients throughout New Mexico

### Primary Practice Focus For This Refactor
Build the site around these four lead practices:

- Divorce
- Criminal Defense
- Personal Injury
- Family Law

The legacy site also references medical malpractice, business disputes, and civil lawsuits. Do not let those secondary areas dominate the new information architecture unless the user later asks for them.

### Source Material To Preserve From Existing Site
Use the existing lawyers505.com site as the primary content and asset reference. Preserve and modernize these factual details where still supported by the source:

- Firm name: Lawyers505.com / Lawyers 505
- Phone: (505) 888-5200
- Address: 2155 Louisiana Blvd. NE, Albuquerque, New Mexico 87110
- Geography: Albuquerque-based, serving clients throughout New Mexico
- Offers: free consultations, same-day appointments, payment plans
- Tone: assertive, reassuring, trial-ready, practical

### Persona And Tone
The brand voice should feel like this:

- Grounded and battle-tested
- Direct, not theatrical
- Reassuring without sounding soft
- Protective of families, rights, and futures
- Local to Albuquerque and credible in New Mexico courts
- Clear enough for a stressed visitor who needs help now

Writing style directives:

- Use short, strong sentences
- Prefer plain English over legal jargon
- Speak in first person plural: We, Our
- Emphasize action, protection, preparation, and results
- Avoid chest-thumping claims that sound unbelievable
- Avoid vague motivational language

### Visual Identity Direction
Use a visual language that feels Southwestern, professional, and courtroom-serious.

Suggested design cues:

- Warm sand, adobe, clay, charcoal, deep desert blue, restrained brass accents
- Strong serif or humanist typography paired with a clean, readable sans
- Photography direction: Albuquerque skyline, courthouse context, attorney portraits, client-trust moments, not generic gavels or handshake stock art
- Layout direction: spacious, editorial, decisive hierarchy, prominent phone CTA
- Motion: minimal and purposeful; confidence over flash

### Historical Context
There is no prior build in this project. The only historical reference is the current public Lawyers 505 website, which appears content-heavy, repetitive, and visually dated. The new site should retain substance while greatly improving clarity, hierarchy, and polish.
