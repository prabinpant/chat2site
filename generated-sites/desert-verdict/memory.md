# The Vision
Build a bold, unconventional refactor of the existing Lawyers505.com website for a law firm in Albuquerque, New Mexico, USA. The finished site must feel distinctive and modern while staying grounded in the real firm identity, real services, and real wording gathered from approved source material only.

This project is a fresh start. No visual assets and no external reference sites have been provided yet. Until source content is collected, do not invent facts, credentials, case results, awards, office details, attorney bios, testimonials, pricing, or promises.

# Persona & Tone
## INTERNAL STRATEGY
The brand voice should feel steady, clear, and human. It should sound like experienced legal counsel speaking directly to people dealing with high-stakes personal situations. The emotional register should balance urgency with calm control.

Design identity should avoid generic legal templates. Use an unconventional layout with strong composition, asymmetry, unexpected section rhythm, oversized typography, and deliberate contrast, while keeping navigation and calls to action simple and trustworthy.

Visual direction should reflect Albuquerque and the Southwest without becoming thematic or decorative. The mood should be grounded, confident, disciplined, and contemporary.

# Site Experience Directives
## INTERNAL STRATEGY
The layout must be unconventional but still usable.

Recommended experience traits:
- Large headline blocks with broken-grid composition
- Alternating dense and quiet sections
- Strong section framing instead of template-like cards everywhere
- Sticky or split-screen moments used carefully
- Distinct mobile behavior that preserves hierarchy without becoming cluttered

The site should feel intentional rather than experimental for its own sake. Legal visitors must still be able to identify services, trust signals, and contact actions immediately.
