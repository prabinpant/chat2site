# Project Memory

## The Vision
Create a polished, contemporary website for Brian's Plumbing Works, doing business as Plumbing-Geek, that feels more refined and visually memorable than the current site while staying tightly grounded in the existing source content. The finished site should present the business as an experienced Portland-area plumbing contractor with unusual depth in vintage plumbing restoration, older homes, fixture repair, remodel work, and practical plumbing knowledge.

The new site should feel trustworthy, skilled, and deeply hands-on. It should not read like a generic plumbing company. It should feel like a specialist who understands both how plumbing works and why old fixtures, historic interiors, and well-made details matter.

## Persona & Tone
### Internal Strategy
Use a voice that feels knowledgeable, plainspoken, and highly experienced. The brand should come across as careful, direct, and unpretentious.

Design identity:
- Historic-meets-practical
- Craftsman workshop energy, not luxury showroom gloss
- Warm metals, old porcelain, enamel, cast iron, patina, and honest tools
- Portland heritage-home sensibility
- Clean layouts with strong typography, clear hierarchy, and generous breathing room

Tone cues:
- Experienced without sounding corporate
- Helpful without sounding chatty
- Precise without sounding technical for its own sake
- Respectful of old homes, old fixtures, and the people who care about them

Visual cues:
- Brass, copper, aged nickel, porcelain white, deep slate, workbench neutrals
- Real materials over abstract tech styling
- Strong before-and-after storytelling if source material supports it
- Details that suggest restoration craft and repair knowledge

## Comparative Research Directives
### Internal Strategy

Capture exact or near-exact factual content for:
- Business name and DBA usage
- Contact details
- About story and years in business
- Service categories
- Vintage fixture restoration capabilities
- Remodel and repair offerings
- Any wording about old houses, preservation, sanitation, safety, or plumbing education

Pull real content, not commentary about the site.

If the source site includes unique phrasing, preserve it closely and only rephrase where needed for clarity, readability, or layout.

Use third-party references only as backup validation when the source site is unclear. Do not let third-party directory copy override the source site.

Recommended page structure to verify against source content:
- Home
- About
- Services
- Vintage Restoration or equivalent specialty page
- Contact
- Optional project/gallery or knowledge section only if supported by source content

## Content Priorities
### Internal Strategy
The strongest differentiator is not general plumbing alone. It is the combination of:
- General plumbing repair and service
- Remodel capability
- Vintage fixture restoration
- Respect for historic homes and original architectural character

The site should keep this specialty front and center. Avoid burying the restoration angle beneath generic plumbing language.

### Intro Section
We work on the everyday plumbing problems that need to be fixed right, and we also take on the older fixtures and restoration work that many plumbers would rather replace. When function matters and the original character of the home matters too, we bring both together.

### About Section
Brian's Plumbing Works, doing business as Plumbing-Geek, has served Portland-area homeowners with plumbing repair, remodel work, and vintage plumbing restoration since 1984. We work on modern plumbing needs, but we are especially known for helping older homes keep the fixtures, details, and character that make them worth preserving.

### Services Overview
**General Plumbing Service**
We diagnose and repair the usual plumbing problems in kitchens, baths, and household systems, with straightforward service focused on getting things working properly again.

**Remodel Plumbing**
We handle plumbing work for remodel projects with attention to fit, function, and the way the finished space is meant to look and feel.

**Vintage Fixture Restoration**
We restore vintage plumbing fixtures so they can be used and enjoyed again, instead of being discarded when the right repair or rebuild can bring them back.

**Fixture Sourcing & Consultation**
We help homeowners select vintage fixtures and make informed decisions about what can be restored, repaired, rebuilt, or installed.

### Vintage Restoration Feature
Old fixtures have a feel, proportion, and craftsmanship that new products rarely match. We restore vintage plumbing fixtures with an eye toward both reliable function and the original look of the piece. That means helping you keep the details that belong in the house instead of replacing them with something that does not.

### Old Homes Feature
Older homes need more than standard plumbing work. They need someone who understands how the systems were built, how fixtures were made, and how repairs affect the look and integrity of the space. We work with that in mind from the start.

### Why Homeowners Call Us
- Experience built over decades of hands-on plumbing work
- Practical repair knowledge for everyday plumbing problems
- Specialized restoration work for vintage fixtures
- Respect for the character and architecture of older homes
- Clear guidance when repair, restoration, or replacement is on the table

## Copy Guardrails
### Internal Strategy
Do:
- Stay close to source wording and facts
- Keep copy grounded, specific, and human
- Let restoration expertise feel distinctive
- Write in first person where appropriate

Do not:
- Add claims not supported by the source site
- Add promotional language about speed, pricing, guarantees, or emergency service unless explicitly confirmed
- Turn the business into a broad corporate plumbing brand
- Dilute the vintage restoration specialty with generic contractor copy
