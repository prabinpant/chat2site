# INTERNAL STRATEGY

## Vision
Build a service-business website that feels immediate, dependable, and locally grounded. The site should present Locksmith Charley as the first call for lockouts, rekeying, lock changes, commercial security work, car key help, and safe services across two active markets: the Phoenix area and Orlando, FL. The experience should feel fast, practical, and professional, with strong local-service intent and clear conversion paths on every page.

The site should balance urgency with credibility. Visitors should quickly understand that we handle everyday emergency locksmith work as well as higher-trust jobs like business access control support, property rekeying, and safe opening or service.

## Core Business Framing
- Primary offer: mobile locksmith services with broad residential, commercial, automotive, and safe capabilities
- Geographic strategy: keep Phoenix-area authority while clearly expanding into Orlando
- Conversion priority: calls first, form leads second
- User intent buckets:
  1. Locked out now
  2. Need rekey or lock replacement
  3. Need commercial locksmith support
  4. Need car key, ignition, or lockout help
  5. Need safe opening, repair, or combination help

## Brand Positioning
Locksmith Charley should feel like a skilled local operator with real field experience, not a generic lead-gen directory. The brand voice should be direct, helpful, calm under pressure, and neighborhood-aware. The design should avoid gimmicks and instead signal practical expertise, mobility, responsiveness, and trust.

## Persona & Tone
### Voice Attributes
- Direct
- Reassuring
- Experienced
- Local
- Action-oriented
- Professional without sounding corporate

### Writing Style
- Short, confident sentences
- Plainspoken service explanations
- Strong local references where relevant
- Avoid hype, fluff, and vague claims
- Prefer specificity: lockouts, rekeying, deadbolts, access solutions, car keys, safes

### Design Identity
- Visual tone: modern local service business with strong utility and polish
- Mood: reliable, fast, clean, grounded
- Suggested palette direction:
  - Deep navy or charcoal for trust and contrast
  - Warm brass, amber, or copper accent to echo locks, keys, and hardware
  - Clean white or sand neutrals for readability
- Typography direction:
  - Bold, condensed or sturdy display type for headlines
  - Highly readable sans-serif for body copy
- Imagery direction:
  - Mobile locksmith at work
  - Van/truck service cues
  - Locks, deadbolts, key cutting, car key programming, safes
  - Residential doors, storefronts, office entries, vehicles
  - Phoenix and Orlando local environmental cues used subtly

Homeowners, renters, property managers, and new home buyers who need lockouts, lock changes, rekeying, deadbolt installation, or door hardware service.

### Commercial
Retail shops, offices, warehouses, property managers, and multi-unit operators who need lock repair, rekeying, master key systems, panic hardware, or access-related support.

### Automotive
Drivers needing lockout service, car key replacement, ignition help, broken key extraction, or transponder/programming support where applicable.

### Safes
Homeowners and businesses needing safe opening, lock repair, combination changes, and general safe servicing.
