# Pacific Northwest X-Ray Memory

## The Vision
Create a clean modern refactor of Pacific Northwest X-Ray Inc. that preserves the company’s established catalog identity, business information, and product-first shopping flow. The new site should feel more organized and easier to navigate while staying faithful to the existing brand, wording, and practical catalog tone.

## Persona & Tone
Pacific Northwest X-Ray Inc. should sound experienced, direct, helpful, and product-focused. The voice is plainspoken and professional, with no hype, no trend language, and no decorative claims. The site should feel like a dependable medical and radiology supply house that helps customers find exactly what they need and place orders quickly.

Design identity:
- Straightforward, credible, industrial-medical, and catalog-led.
- Clean layouts, clear product grouping, strong contact visibility, and easy access to ordering information.
- Functional and trustworthy rather than editorial or promotional.
- Preserve the established Pacific Northwest X-Ray business name and Gresham, Oregon location.

## Historical Context
This is a fresh project. No new site has been built yet.

## Information Architecture Guidance
Build the site like a modern catalog storefront, not a campaign site.

Primary page/content priorities:
- Home
- Product search / catalog access
- Popular categories
- Ordering information
- Policies
- Contact details

Recommended homepage structure:
- Header with company name, product search, and direct contact access
- Hero with the established catalog line
- Popular product/category grid using real homepage link titles
- Order/contact strip with phone, toll-free, fax, and hours
- Catalog notice and business footer

## Content Handling Rules
- When a line already exists on the source site and fits the new UI, keep it verbatim.
- Product descriptions, policy statements, and ordering details should be lifted from source pages with wording intact.
- If the legacy site contains awkward phrasing, preserve meaning and prefer exact reuse unless the build absolutely requires a shorter label for navigation.
- Navigation labels may be simplified only when they point to unchanged source content.
